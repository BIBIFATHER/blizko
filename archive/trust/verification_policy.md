# Trust Policy — Verification v1

## Definition: “Verified nanny”
Required:
- At least 1 identity document uploaded
- Phone verified
- Manual review completed (A/B/C triage)

## Red flags (manual review)
- Mismatched name/ID
- Suspicious phone/email patterns
- Missing document metadata

## Escalation
- Safety risk → Trust lead within 12h
- Data inconsistency → Ops lead within 24h
