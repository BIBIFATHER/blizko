# 2026-05-18

## Blizko Redesign Direction

- User clarified the core redesign lens: the parent arrives tired, anxious, low-attention, often in the evening. The interface must not demand reading, comparison, or many decisions.
- Canonical emotional promise:
  - "Я сказал, что у нас происходит. Меня поняли. Мне спокойно показали следующий шаг."
  - Short formula: remove everything that makes a tired parent think; add everything that creates the feeling that they were understood, the choice was narrowed, and the next step is safe.
- Launch priority changed / locked:
  - first major implementation should be `Home + first step of ParentForm`;
  - do not start with `MatchResults` or palette polish.
- Home must stop acting like a landing page:
  - one strong thesis;
  - one CTA;
  - short trust line;
  - quick-start chips.
- Parent flow must start with story, not filters:
  - "Расскажите, что сейчас нужно семье";
  - large calm field;
  - chips such as "нужна помощь вечером", "сложный график", "важен мягкий подход", "ребёнок тревожится".
- Product must show "we understood" summaries after story and key steps.
- Match results should be a curated 2-3 nanny shortlist, not a catalog.
- Remove AI hype:
  - avoid "AI-анализ", "алгоритм", "Humanity+ technology";
  - use "мы подобрали", "куратор посмотрит", "почему подходит", "что дальше".
- SuccessScreen should become a calm curator handoff:
  - "Спасибо. Мы поняли контекст."
  - "Куратор посмотрит заявку."
  - "Вы получите короткий shortlist."
  - "Можно вернуться позже."
- Added `.context/REDESIGN_2_WEEK_LAUNCH_ROOM.md` as the operational source for the two-week frontend relaunch.
